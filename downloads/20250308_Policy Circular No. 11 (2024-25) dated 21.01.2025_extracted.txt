Subject: EPCG Scheme - Relief in Average EO in terms of the para 5.17(a) of Hand Book of
Procedures (HBP) of FTP, 2023.

The para 5.17 of the HBP of the FTP, 2023 envisages that to provide relief to exporters of
those sectors where total exports in that sector/product group has declined by more than 5% as
compared to the previous year, the Average Export Obligation (EO) for the year may be reduced
proportionate to reduction in exports of that particular sector/product group during the relevant year
as against the preceding year. This implies that the sector/product group that witnessed such decline
in 2023-24 as compared to 2022-23 would be entitled for such relief.
